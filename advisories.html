ANNOUNCEMENT: DRPS PHCA will remain online this Holy Week 2025.

4/14/2025

0 Comments

 
​In observance of the Holy Week from April 17 to 19, 2025, the DRPS PHCA is still committed to continue to provide TV, Radio, and Online Ad Archives in the Philippines through DRPS One, DRPS Two, DRPS Three, DRPS Xtra, and DRPS PHCA TVAL Video YouTube Channels (also for DRPS One's Facebook and X/Twitter Pages).

Our online media pages, such as the DRPS PHCA Social Media Accounts (on Facebook, X/Twitter, Threads, and Instagram), the DRPS PHCA Free Facebook Community Group, the DRPS PHCA Website (which includes DRPS PHCA Free Blog and Weekly Newsletter, PBB S10 Teens/Gen 11/Collab Blogs, and DRPS PHCA Check) will continue to go online this Paschal Triduum Period.

Moreover, from April 17 to 18, 2025, in accordance with the COMELEC Resolution no. 10999, involving the 2025 National and Local, Midterm Elections, the DRPS One, DRPS Two, and DRPS PHCA TVAL Channels will have no Electoral Propaganda archives for TV and Radio, as well as commercial break videos with campaign ads on each gap, as campaigning on these dates are prohibited this Holy Week. It will resume it's publishings from April 19, 2025 until the end of the campaign period.

Also for PBB Collab fans, while the Online Blog for this edition of PBB Celebrity Edition will remain online, the reactions part on our X Account will also not be available from April 17 to 19, 2025, as this reality show will not be shown on-air on these dates. We will resume publishing from April 20, 2025 onwards.

Have a blessed Holy Week, from DRPS PHCA - Color Your World.

Advisory: DRPS PHCA during the Holy Week

4/4/2025

0 Comments

 
​The DRPS Philippine Commercial Archives ("DRPS PHCA") will remain online this coming lenten season, from April 17 to 19, 2025. The DRPS PHCA will continue to post a number of content on our YouTube Channels 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, specifically the DRPS One (including on Facebook and X/Twitter), DRPS Two, DRPS Three, DRPS Xtra, and DRPS PHCA TV Ad Listings Video Channels, the DRPS PHCA Free Website, Online Blog, Weekly Newsletter, and Community Group, and the DRPS PHCA Pinoy Big Brother (PBB) Kumuniteens, Gen 11, and Celebrity Collab Edition Housemate Updates, Blogs, and etc.

Moreover, some of our services that are published on our social media accounts, will not be available for a brief period of time within the Paschal Triduum, as described by the following:

DRPS PHCA Politicalism 2025 (Political TV and Radio Ad Archive): It will not be available this April 17 and 18, 2025 on DRPS One, DRPS Two, and DRPS PHCA TVAL Video Channels, in accordance with the COMELEC's Campaigning Policy within the campaigning period for both National and Local Positions. It will resume on April 19, 2025, Black Saturday.
DRPS PHCA PBB Collab Reactions (on DRPS PHCA's X Account): It will not be available on April 17 to 19, 2025, as this reality show will not be broadcast within the Paschal Triduum, despite it's all-access streaming on the iWantTFC App and Website will remain available. It will resume on April 20, 2025, Easter Sunday.

Thank you.

ADVISORY: DRPS PHCA on the support of PBB Kumuniteens and Gen 11 upon the premiere of Celebrity Collab Edition

3/10/2025

0 Comments

 

​We will still support the housemates of Pinoy Big Brother: Season 10 Teen Edition and Gen 11, alongside with the latest edition called Celebrity Collab Edition. Thank you for your continued trust with DRPS PHCA.

​We're pleased to inform that the DRPS PHCA will continue to support the PBB Kumuniteens and Gen 11 at this time, aside from the recently premiered Celebrity Collab Edition, as a token of appreciation of our viewers, followers, and supporters of every housemate of these editions of Pinoy Big Brother. As a result, the DRPS PHCA will remain to be a proud supporter of these two editions of PBB.

The Pinoy Big Brother: Season 10 Teen Edition, widely known as "Kumuniteens", and monikered as "The Kumuniteens Housemates of Big Brother", that premiered almost three years ago (March 13, 2022) consists of these thirteen (13) housemates, such as Rob Blackburn and Luke Alford, as seen on ASAP under "Rockoustic Heartthrobs", Dustine Mayores and Maxine Trinidad, widely known for Pamilya Sagrado that premiered last June 17, 2024, Tiff Ronato and Kai Espenido, known for their pageantry winnings, such as for Tiff, Ms. SCUAA Region 8 2023 and Mutya San Allen 2023, and for Kai, Mutya Hong Butuan 2024 and Miss FIT Philippines 2024, Gabb Birkin, known for being seen on BingoPlus as a host, as well as the other housemates of this edition - Ashton Salvador, Stef Draper, Eslam el Gohari, Stephanie Jordan, Paolo Alcantara, and Don Hilario. On the last day of that edition, Gabb and Rob were named as the Top 2 Housemates, with Rob Blackburn was hailed as the fourth big placer of PBB Season 10 as a whole, during the Biga-10 na Big Night last May 29, 2022.

The Pinoy Big Brother: Gen 11, widely known as "New Gen Housemates", and monikered as "The New Gen Housemates of Big Brother", that premiered last July 20, 2024, which consists of twenty (20) housemates, particularly JM Ibarra, Fyang Smith, Kai Montinola, Rain Celmar, Jarren Garcia, Kolette Madelo, Dingdong Bahan and Patrick Ramirez (DongPat), Dylan Yturralde, Jas Dudley-Scales, Joli Alferez and Gwen Montano (Joli-Gwen), JP Cabrera, Therese Villamor, Binsoy Namoca, Kanata Tapia, Marc Nanninga, Jan Silva, Brx Ruiz, and Noimie Steikunas, that stood it's overall dominance, naming Kai, Kolette and Rain as their Big Placers, and Fyang as the Big Winner of this edition, including the record-breaking Peak Concurrent Viewership record of 2.26 Million Viewers on Kapamilya Online Live, and their breakthrough features on various TV Shows (i.e. It's Showtime, ASAP, Kuan on One S2) and among others), Music Videos (i.e. Rainbow Christmas, Wherever You Are, and Ako Na Sana), and many others.

As DRPS PHCA will provide coverage involving the latest edition of PBB, known as Celebrity Collab Edition, as seen on-air via GMA, and online via Kapuso Stream and Kapamilya Online Live, the media entity will remain the proud supporter of the past two editions before it - Season 10 Teens and Gen 11.
