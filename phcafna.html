We have seen an another TikTok user (this time by the handle of "albertbacs") claiming that the country's Queen of All Media, Kris Aquino, was passed away - in which he claimed that she's born in the year 1978 - which is wrong (the right one that she was born in February 14, 1971, that's 53 years old as of this date).

We have also received a report via DRPS PHCA Discord Server, who have shared a website pertaining to a video regarding that there's an exact date that Kris Aquino will pass away, as seen from a video via SHAREit's domain name (an online file sharing platform).

We have warned the public last week regarding to these concerns, and we wish to inform that ALL OF THE SPECULATIONS REGARDING "KRIS AQUINO IS PASSING AWAY", whether it is for a conspiracy, or a social media post/video on the Internet (i.e. TikTok, and other SNS Platforms, like Facebook, X, Instagram, and etc.), are ALL FALSE, MISLEADING, AND A DEATH HOAX.

Fake News is a cybercrime - DON'T BE FOOLED. Think it and research it before you can interact with a post or share a news article with your friends, family, or someone else. And for the case of Kris Aquino, as of this date, she is still alive as of the time in the making of this post.

We encourage you to beware of these news, just like these two. Thank you for understanding.
