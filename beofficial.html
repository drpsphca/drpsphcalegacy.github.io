To report a fake YouTube Channels of DRPS PHCA:

  1. Go to any of the YouTube Channels that impersonates our channels (for example, a fake DRPS One Channel was displayed that uses their YouTube Channel Handle, "@DRP50ne" instead of the original's "@DRPSOne"
  2. Click on "About"
  3. On the Report user window, Select "Impersonation" on the list.
  4. Under "Impersonation", select "This channel is impersonating someone else" from the drop-down menu then click next.
  5. Under the "What channel is being impersonated?" text box, copy the following Channel URLs below and paste it on the text box then click "next".
  6. Add any additional notes related to the report if necessary and click "Submit".
  7. Your report has been submitted to YouTube.

To report a fake Facebook Pages of DRPS PHCA:

  1. Go to any of the Facebook Pages that impersonates our channels (for example, a fake DRPS PHCA Facebook Page was displayed that uses a page URL any other than the official one, https://www.facebook.com/DRPSPHCAOfficial
  2. Click on the three small dots from the Facebook Page.
  3. Click on "Find support or report" from the list.
  4. From the Report window, click on "Pretending to be something", then select "A business" from the list who was affected in this report (for instance, you are reporting a fake DRPS PHCA Facebook page).
  5. Under the "Facebook Page name or URL" text box, type in "DRPS Philippine Commercial Archives" (for DRPS PHCA) or "DRPS One Channel" (for DRPS One Channel) and look for our pages in here and click "Send"
  6. Your report has been submitted to Facebook. You also have the option to block or hide all of the posts from the impersonating page.

To report a fake Twitter Page of DRPS PHCA:

  1. Go to any of the Twitter Pages that impersonates our channels (for example, a fake DRPS PHCA Twitter Page was displayed that uses a page URL any other than the official one, @DRPSPHCA
  2. Click on the three small dots from the Twitter Page.
  3. Click on the "Report" button from the list and click "Start Report".
  4. From the question "Who is this report for?", select "Everyone on Twitter" then click next.
  5. From the question "What is happening to everyone on Twitter?", select "They're being impersonated or shown a deceptive identity" then click next.
  6. From the question "How is (Twitter Username) doing this?", select "Pretending to be them" then click next.
  7. Under the text box, search for @DRPSPHCA and click on our account, then click next.
  8. From the question "Do you represent @DRPSPHCA?", select "No" then click next.
  9. As you are making a report for impersonation of a Twitter account, read what impersonation does on their policies, and what is allowed in here. Click "Yes, continue" if you read the entire rules.
  10. Review and submit your report. You can also have the option to add other tweets related to the impersonating account and/or additional context related to it, and click "Submit".
  11. ​Your report has been sent to Twitter.

To report a fake Instagram Page of DRPS PHCA:

  1. Go to any of the Instagram Pages that impersonates our channels (for example, a fake DRPS PHCA Instagram Page was displayed that uses a page URL any other than the official one, @drpsphca
  2. Click on the three small dots from the Instagram Page.
  3. Click on the "Report" button from the list and click on "Report Account".
  4. Click on the "It's pretending to be someone else" button from the list then click next.
  5. From the question "Who is this account pretending to be?", select "A business or organization" then click "Submit Report".
  6. Under the text box, search for "DRPS PHCA" and click on our account.
  7. ​Your report has been sent to Instagram.
If you do not have an Instagram account (i.e. you only have a Facebook Account), and you are trying to report an account from here, click here to submit a report and follow the on-screen instructions. Be sure to select "Someone created an account pretending to be me or a friend" from the list.
