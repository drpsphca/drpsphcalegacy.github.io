DRPS PHCA "I-Boycott Ang Peke" Campaign FAQ

What is I-Boycott ang Peke campaign?
The DRPS PHCA's "I-Boycott ang Peke" campaign is our own public advocacy to fight impersonation of YouTube Channels and Social Media pages, and to encourage our viewers, subscribers and followers of all of the DRPS PHCA's channels to only trust our official pages and accounts. This campaign will help our viewers to compare the official DRPS PHCA's YouTube Channels with the fake one.
Why did you create the Channel Status page?
DRPS PHCA created the Channel Status page under the Anti-Impersonation Campaign webpage so viewers who are planning to subscribe the official channels of DRPS One, DRPS Two, DRPS Three, DRPS Xtra, and DRPS PHCA on YouTube can compare how many subscribers and the number of YouTube Videos uploaded that we made with the impersonator's YouTube Channels, aside from checking the channel URL and the channel creation date.

If you have any problems within this page or if you are in doubt, feel free to contact us throughout the page, or via Facebook Messenger within any of the pages of this website.
What to do if someone is impersonating a social media pages that were not owned by drps phca?
If someone impersonates a YouTube Channel and/or a Social Media page that were not related to DRPS PHCA (i.e. DRPS PHCA, DRPS One, DRPS Two, DRPS Three, DRPS Xtra, and etc.) or our affiliate Philippine Television Archives, you might need to search for the official account and compare with the impersonating one (like the number of subscribers, videos, all-time views, page's URL, and the account creation date). If the channel impersonated it, you may report the channel/page as soon as possible.

If you are in doubt, feel free to send us a message through a Private PM on Facebook Messenger that can be found on any part of the page of this website. You can also ask their official social media accounts to verify if an account was an impersonation or not.
If the page is from DRPS PHCA, how to know that a YouTube/Social Media page is Official?
For YouTube, to compare the DRPS PHCA's Official YouTube Channels (i.e. DRPS One, DRPS Two, DRPS Three, and DRPS Xtra) with the impersonating one, aside from checking the channel URL and the account creation date (or channel creation date) as prescribed in the Anti-Impersonation page, you need to visit our own tool - the Channel Status page.

With this page, you can compare the following parameters which one is the real YouTube Channel and which one is the fake of it:
  • The current number of subscribers of a specific DRPS PHCA YouTube Channel
  • The current number of videos uploaded on each channel
  • The current number of views on each channel, across all of the videos uploaded on it

For other social media pages apart from YouTube, such as Facebook, to determine each DRPS PHCA Social Media Page is a legitimate account/page, check the account page's URL, the current number of followers, the number of accounts we followed on our pages, and etc. This will determine which DRPS PHCA Social Media page is real or fake. If the account you visited is official, you can follow, interact, or ask with us with these page. Otherwise, if it is impersonating, do not follow or interact with it, and you may need to report it. The instructions can be found in the Anti-Impersonation Campaign Home Page (click here).
Why you created this campaign?
We are committed to create the I-Boycott ang Peke Campaign to the main public to fight channel and social media page impersonations. This campaign will help us terminate any of their accounts who impersonate DRPS PHCA or someone else's accounts, and to only trust their official accounts.

Impersonating accounts of DRPS PHCA (most commonly faking the official DRPS One Channel) are mostly not related to our archive, reuploading our contents from our channels, vandalizing our name, logo, graphics, and etc., and among others are the ones that each person is doing this, so we created this campaign to cope impersonation of social media accounts and YouTube Channels, as in accordance with their respective social media page's Community Guidelines and its related policies.
What do you mean by Community Guidelines on YouTube?
The YouTube's own Community Guidelines is a policy to check what type of content, channel, or related portions, is strictly prohibited, including impersonation.

​The Impersonation Policy is a part of the Community Guidelines, and violations of this policy will lead to immediate channel termination without a warning or a strike, even it is a single case of severe abuse of the site, or for dedicating a specific terminated channel for violating a policy. This is also a part of the site's Terms of Service.
Are the logos, images, graphics, and etc. of DRPS PHCA Copyrighted?
The DRPS PHCA's logo, images, graphics and etc. are created and owned by DRPS PHCA. You are free to use our materials related to DRPS PHCA, but please note that each content must follow our Terms of Use and it's Contents and Comments policy as amended here, as these were copyrighted and owned by DRPS PHCA. In particular, you should respect DRPS PHCA's logo, name, and related content or else, we will delete your content on your video.

Not following the Contents and Comments policy (i.e. unauthorized use, manipulation, and etc.) will lead to a Copyright Takedown against your YouTube Video. If the channel used our real channel logo as our profile picture, we may issue a Copyright Takedown against it. If a channel uses our name, logo, and cover photo, we will report it, which you should also do it as well.
What is YouTube Channel Impersonation on DRPS PHCA?
Channels impersonating a specific DRPS PHCA Channel (mostly fake DRPS One Channel) are mostly posts videos that were not related to our official channels or sometimes they might reupload our contents from the channel. In order cases, they might be bullies against us (i.e. various scandals related to the channels owned by Jester Buknoy) by posting unpleasant comments, posts or videos on these channels that were unaffiliated with DRPS PHCA.

These channel impersonators should be stopped and therefore, the YouTube's own Report Button was the solution.
I am an owner of a specific channel that uses your name, and i got an email from DRPS PHCA, informing that I impersonated your channel. What should I do?
In some cases, we may send you an email against your YouTube Channel because it was found out that you used our channel name, description, profile picture and cover photo without your permission and we have flagged your impersonating channel using the YouTube's Report Button. If in case you received that email, we urge you to close your channel or make some changes on it (like changing your profile photo and/or cover photo with your own, updating your channel name, handle, and description, and etc.)
I received a copyright takedown notice from YouTube and I've just read that the copyright owner was DRPS PHCA. What is it and why this happened to my channel?
Unfortunately, in order to fight not just channel impersonation of DRPS PHCA or any contents that were unrelated to DRPS PHCA, we may issue a Copyright Takedown Strike against any of your channel's video, if the content violates our Contents and Comments policy that it is part of our Terms of Use of this website, wherein the use of DRPS PHCA's Copyrighted work (i.e. the logo, cover photo, profile picture, and etc.)

If you received a copyright takedown that has been issued by DRPS PHCA for the first time (at least one and/or more strikes), or if your channel was hacked, please delete your video and ask us for a retraction request through our email or private message on Facebook Messenger. By retracting, you should understand that you will not use our logo again. The next time you did this again, we will not retract it.

Please note: Beware of any false Copyright Takedown Strike emails claiming to be YouTube or from DRPS PHCA, so please check the email address and its domain to make sure that the email you're receiving is from is an official message.
Are any legacy custom channel URLs of DRPS PHCA-specific YouTube Channel considered official? What about a non-DRPS PHCA Channel?
If you clicked, seen or browse a specific DRPS PHCA YouTube Channel with our legacy custom channel URL link displayed prior to November 2022 (for example, for the DRPS One Channel, the legacy channel URL is ​https://www.youtube.com/c/DRPSOnePhilippineTVCArchives), it is considered an official channel, just to make sure that it has the same parameters prescribed in our Channel Status page.

Aside from DRPS PHCA, if someone has their legacy channel URL (including with channel URLs containing the "user" on it, i.e. https://www.youtube.com/user/YouTube) prior to its introduction of YouTube Handles and its automatic assignment (if not done so as early as November 14, 2022), you may subscribe, interact, and watch their legitimate videos, just to make sure that has their official check mark at the right part of their channel's name or it has their own official social media pages, if it doesn't have a check mark.
I have seen a Social Media page outside of YouTube, Facebook, or any other sites you have that has been created by DRPS PHCA, but someone created an account that uses your name on their account, username, and etc. What should I do?
Unfortunately, if someone used DRPS PHCA's name, username, logo, and etc. on other Social Media Pages any other than the ones that DRPS PHCA has (these are YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Rumble, DRPS PHCA Website, Kumu, Tiktok (Deprecated Account), Wikipedia (OGCWiki), Patreon, Wikia (drpsbn), and Miraheze), it might be likely that this was a fake account. If you have seen any of these accounts that uses our name and has been created on any other platforms than the ones we have as mentioned previously, please consult us through our email or via our private message. We will take actions on these accounts as soon as possible, if any.
Does the DRPS Two Channel has a Join button, and what is it?
The short answer is yes, our own Radio Ad Archive Channel, DRPS Two Channel, does have a join button, where you can support the archive with a monthly subscription. If you see this button, whether you subscribed to that channel or not, this is an official DRPS Two Channel, and you are free to make a monthly payment - thus more members means more improvements on us, apart from watching our Radio Ad videos as well.

If you did not see the Join Button on DRPS Two Channel, please check the Channel Handle you are viewing to make sure that it is a real channel (being sure that the handle "@DRPSTwo" was displayed on the channel page). If it is not, its best not to subscribe to that channel, and we encourage you to subscribe our official Radio Ad Channel of DRPS Two.
I'm not sure if a YouTube Channel or a Social Media Page is real or fake, or I have any other questions not listed in this FAQ, What to do?
If you are not sure if a specific YouTube Channel/Social Media Page is an official account or not, please reach us directly through our social media pages or via our email.
